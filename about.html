    About Us

    The Magazine

    The Edge Magazine provides information about sustainable practices, and social and environmental issues relevant to the Thompson Okanagan region. Our articles consist of both original, local contributions and relevant content curated from around the web.

    Topics we cover include:

    • Organic gardening
    • Permaculture
    • Homesteading
    • Green practices
    • Environmentalism
    • Social activism
    • Political movements
    • Ethical business
    • Climate change
    • Current events
    • Grass roots solutions
    • Scientific solutions
    • Political solutions
    • Multimedia reviews

    Resources

    We also provide links and information related to resources that may be of interest, including:

    • Activist groups
    • Support groups
    • Local food growers and breeders
    • Retailers

    Event Calendar

    We provide a listing of local events provided by our readers. If you have an event that may be of interest and relevant to the topics listed above, submit it in the events area to have it added to the list. We accept events taking place within the Thompson Okanagan region, but we may list an event outside of that area if it is significantly large enough to attract a great many people.

    Contributions

    We want to hear from our readers. If you have an opinion, information, or experience you would like to share with us, check our submission guidelines. The more local articles we can provide, the more locally relevant our magazine becomes!

    Contact Us

    If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions, please contact us.
