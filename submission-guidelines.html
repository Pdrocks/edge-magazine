    How to Make a Contribution

    Submit your contribution.

    What we accept

    We accept contributions of original articles between 400 to 1000 words in length. The subject matter must fit our content criteria as per the “topics we publish” list on the About page. Your contribution can be a personal narrative of your own experiences, an expression of your opinions, a researched essay, a news article, or a how-to guide. It can include images, video or both. We also accept reviews of books, documentaries, podcasts, music, or other media directly related to our content criteria.

    Be clear and concise

    The more easily digestible your work is, the more likely it is to be accepted. Use plain language and state your point up front. Explain yourself in precise terms. If you must use jargon or complex vocabulary to make your point, provide definitions. Avoid meandering text, omit needless words, and keep your word count low.

    Cite your work

    All researched work must be correctly cited according to Chicago Manual of Style. Alternatively, you may provide links to references within the work itself.

    Regarding images and video

    All images and video must be original. Ownership of images and video remains with you; we will not reproduce, distribute, or share any content you upload to us beyond the publication of your contribution.

    How to upload files

    Text files must be in PDF format. They must not include images. Instead, indicate in the text where images should be placed.

    Video files must be in MP4 format. Videos uploaded to us must be 10 minutes or less in length. If you prefer, you may upload your videos to YouTube or Vimeo and provide us with the link.

    Image files must be in one of the following formats:

    • gif
    • png
    • jpeg

    Article images must be no more than 696px wide. Profile and event images must be 400px by 400px. Send us the high resolution versions.

    To upload:

    • Compress your files (PC help) (Mac help).
    • Include your name in the name of your zip file.
    • Locate the zip file with the browse button.
    • And upload!

    What to expect

    When you submit, you will be asked to provide a summary of your contribution. This should be a brief description (2 - 5 sentences) outlining your contribution's content. You will also be asked to provide us with a bio of 40 - 50 words in length. If we have already published your bio, and you do not wish to update it, you may enter "Bio on file" in the Bio section of the form. If you wish, you may submit a photo or other image to represent you. This image should be 400px by 400px in size.

    Your contribution will then be reviewed. Please allow us up to 72 hours to respond. We will either accept and publish your contribution, or provide you with feedback. You may be asked to revise and resubmit.

    If accepted, your contribution will be published. We may add headings or images to the presentation of your work, but we will not alter the text. Please edit carefully.

    Ownership remains with you; we will not reproduce, distribute, or share any content you upload to us beyond the publication of your contribution. Any requests to reproduce your work will be forwarded to you. If, at any time, you wish to have your content removed from the site, contact us and we will do so.

    Please be aware that we are not responsible for any unauthorized third party distribution of your contribution.
